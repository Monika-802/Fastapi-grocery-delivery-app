1.sAPI Endpoints:

GET /items – Retrieve all items.
GET /items/{item_id} – Retrieve a single item by ID.
POST /items – Add a new item.
PUT /items/{item_id} – Update an item’s price or stock status.
DELETE /items/{item_id} – Delete an item.
GET /items/filter – Filter items by category, price range, unit, or stock.
GET /items/search – Search items by keyword (name or category).
GET /items/sort – Sort items by any field (price, name, id) in ascending or descending order.
GET /items/page – Paginate items (default 4 per page).
GET /items/browse – Combine keyword search, category filter, stock filter, sort, and pagination.
GET /items/summary – Summary of items with in-stock/out-of-stock count and category breakdown.

2. Order Management:

POST /orders/place – Place an order with calculation of total cost, delivery charges, and bulk discounts.
POST /orders/create – Create an order without modifying global orders list (returns calculated amounts).
GET /orders – Retrieve all orders.
GET /orders/search – Search orders by customer name.
GET /orders/sort – Sort orders by total_cost.
GET /orders/page – Paginate orders.
Business Logic:
Bulk orders (quantity >=10) receive 8% discount.
Delivery charges: Morning ₹40, Evening ₹60, Others ₹0.

3. Shopping Cart
POST /cart/add – Add items to cart (increments quantity if item already exists).
GET /cart – View cart items and total.
DELETE /cart/{item_id} – Remove a specific item from cart.
POST /cart/checkout – Place all cart items as confirmed orders and clear the cart.
Helper Functions
find_item(item_id) – Returns item dictionary by ID.
calculate_total(item, quantity) – Returns subtotal for a given item and quantity.
filter_items_logic(category, min_price, max_price, unit, in_stock) – Returns filtered list of items.
calculate_order_total(price, quantity, delivery_slot, bulk_order) – Returns detailed cost breakdown including discounts and delivery charges.
